About Mizu

Mizu Fine Dining is an ultra-luxury Japanese restaurant concept located in Victoria Island, Lagos.

Designed for 40–60 exclusive seats, the restaurant delivers a premium omakase-led experience, blending the precision of modern Japanese cuisine with the sophistication of Lagos' high-net-worth clientele.

Mizu positions itself as the most refined Japanese dining destination in Nigeria

Brand Story

Mizu means ‘water’ in Japanese, symbolizing purity, calm, flow, and depth.

The brand is built around Zen minimalism, water-inspired aesthetics, and world-class culinary artistry.

The restaurant features an omakase room, robata grill, a premium sake and whisky library, and an open sushi bar for immersive dining.
